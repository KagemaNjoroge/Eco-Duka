Our Services

What we do

For end users

Browse and Select a Product

Explore our Marketplace and discover a diverse range of products tailored to your needs and preferences. Whether you're looking for solar panels, solar lamps, or electric bikes, we've got you covered. Take your time to find the perfect solution for your sustainable energy needs.

Choose a Payment Plan

Select a payment plan that fits your budget and lifestyle. With flexible options like daily, weekly, or monthly installments, you can enjoy the benefits of clean energy without breaking the bank. Choose the plan that works best for you and proceed to the next step with confidence.

Pay the Deposit

Secure your chosen product by making a deposit payment through our secure payment gateway. Whether you prefer to use your credit/debit card, mobile money, or bank transfer, rest assured that your transaction is safe and protected. Your journey to sustainable living starts here.

Receive the Product and Start Payment

Once your deposit is confirmed, it's time to receive your product and kickstart your journey towards a greener future. Arrange for pickup or delivery, and start making regular payments according to your chosen payment schedule. With each payment, you'll be one step closer to owning your renewable energy solution outright. Let's make sustainable living a reality, together.
